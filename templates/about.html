MOUNTS


MOUNTS is a near real-time monitoring system of volcanoes and earthquakes, based on the analysis of Sentinel data.


The project is funded by GEO.X and lead by Sébastien Valade, in partnership with TU-Berlin (Computer Vision & Remote Sensing group) and GFZ (Physics of Earthquakes and Volcanoes section).
